FairyLand

This product is a 2D narrative adventure side-scroller game. The game's background is founded on the spiritual world of a person in vegetative state. The themes for three missions in this game are friendship, kinship, and love. The player needs to control the main character to interact with other characters in the surrounding to acquire achievements, unlocking different endings.

Level 1 Friendship

The NPC in the first level represent friends, and the main character has to help each other with his friends to help them pass the obstacles and then go to the end together.

In Mission 1, when the MC arrives at the designated location, the NPC will start following the MC. NPC’s movement is bound to the MC, There are some obstacles existing on the NPC’s pathfindings and routes. The NPC will need help from the MC to clear the obstacles and keep moving. When the MC jumps, and NPC will jump too. After the NPC helps the MC to pass all obstacles and challenges, and meet all collection requirements, some changes will occur to the NPC.

Level 2 Kinship

The second level of the NPC represents the family, the family will help the main character through the obstacles, and the further forward, the slower the family will be, the main character should wait patiently for the family to move forward together.

In Mission 2,the NPC will be waiting for the MC next to the tree. It requires the NPC to knock down the wooden board to help the MC to pass. After crossing the wooden bridge, the NPC’s movement speed will decrease. If the distance between the MC and NPC is less then 800 meters, it means the MC is waiting for the NPC. If the MC awaits for the NPC to show up, the NPC will then watch the MC leaving from the stairs. After meet all collection requirement, the small ball will turn yellow. On the contrary, NPC will disappear.

Level 3 Love

The NPC in the third level represents the lover, the main character has to move forward with the lover, light the lamp together and walk to the end together.

In Mission 3, MC and NPC have the same motions and location. This mission requires the MC and NPC to light up the lamp together. Lighting up all three lamps is the collection requirement for this level. After all lamps are lighted up, the MC and NPC need to reach the shore on boat.After meet all collection requirements, the small ball will turn pink.

Game End

End 1: This ending will be triggered after three levels of collection conditions are reached. At the end of the ending, along with the sound of the hospital instruments, the main character will wake up in the real world.
End 2: Failure to achieve the three levels of the collection conditions will trigger another end, the main character will return to the beginning of the game into a deep sleep, waiting to wake up again.
